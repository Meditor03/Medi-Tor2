Frequently Asked Questions

what does telemedicine means?

Remote medical services, diagnosing and treating
patients without consulting them in persons, is called
telemedicine. Telemedicine also called online doctor visits.

How can I maintain good health?

Maintaining good health involves adopting a healthy
lifestyle, which includes regular exercise, eating a
balanced diet, getting enough sleep, managing stress,
and avoiding harmful substances such as tobacco
and excessive alcohol.

What is preventive care?

Preventive care refers to medical services aimed at
preventing illnesses and promoting wellness.
This includes routine checkups, screenings,
immunizations, and counseling to help individuals
make healthy lifestyle choices.

How safe is online medical consultation?

the online doctor consultation is entirely safe All your
information, medical records, and prescription are
private and secured.

What tools are needed for telemedicine?

Remote vital monitoring, virtual stethoscope,
wireless scale, Digital otoscope, pulse oximeter,
thermometer
